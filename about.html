ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Samuel Marks and I research and design beautiful user experiences. I hold a Bachelors of Science from the University of Washington College of Engineering, where I studied Human Centered Design & Engineering with a focus on Human Computer Interaction. And I have two years of industry experience as a UX Designer. 


I was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest. I enjoy spending time with my family, reading, playing guitar, and downhill skiing.

 

My interests include cognitive and social neuroscience, music, film, photography, and emerging tech.I am also huge fan of Disney/Pixar animation, storytelling, and coffee!!!
